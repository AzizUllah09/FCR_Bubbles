// 01 — Who we are

Not just a wash. A ritual.

There's a moment, right after a proper wash, when you walk back to your car and pause. The paint catches the light differently. The wheels look sharper. The air inside hits cleaner. That moment is what we chase, every car, every shift.

FCR Bubbles started with a simple rule: wash every car the way we'd wash our own. That means real foam, not watered-down soap. Real microfibers, swapped between cars. Real hands moving over real panels — no automated brushes scratching paint.

We're a Jeddah operation. Loud yellow bays, hexagon lights overhead, and a phone that rings straight through to the people doing the work. No call centers. No upsells. Just clean cars and honest pricing.

If we wouldn't put our own car through it, we won't put yours through it either.
— FCR Bubbles Crew

// 03 — How it works

Four moves. One showroom finish.

01

Roll In

Pull into a clean, well-lit bay. Tell us what you want, hand over the keys — or sit in your car if you'd rather watch the work.

02

Foam It

Pre-rinse, then a thick foam blanket from the cannon. The foam dwells, lifts dirt, and softens grime — so the wash mitt never grinds particles into your paint.

03

Hand Detail

Soft mitts, two-bucket method. Wheels, trim, badges — each handled separately with the right tool. Then a hand-dry with plush microfiber, no streaks, no swirls.

04

Roll Out

Walk around with us. If anything's off, we fix it. Then you pay, slide back behind the wheel, and roll out looking like the day you bought it.
